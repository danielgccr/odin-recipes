"El Bar": Bravas Sauce

Description

One of the most common 'tapas' in any Spanish "bar" is the "patatas bravas", fried potato with a sauce, usually bit spicy. In this recipe we will just make the sauce, which can be a staple for your burgers, pizzas, steaks and more.

Ingredients:

  • 60 ml of extra virgin oil (link)
  • Five garlic cloves (link)
  • One tablespoon of wheat flour (link)
  • Half onion (link)
